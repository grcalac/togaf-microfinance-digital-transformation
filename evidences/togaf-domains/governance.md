# Governance Domain Evidence

## Public summary

The governance domain references architecture standards and security practices such as BIAN, Clean Architecture and OWASP. The article identifies the need for formal architectural review and measurable control coverage.

## Public gap

The AS-IS diagnosis identifies absence of a formal Architecture Review Board process and absence of a measurable OWASP control inventory.

## Public governance expectation

The TO-BE proposal expects formal review before production changes, traceability of design decisions and measurable coverage of security controls.

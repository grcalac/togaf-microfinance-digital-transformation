# Ethical Evidence Publication Policy

## Public repository principle

The entire repository is public. Therefore, every artifact must be safe for unrestricted reader and reviewer access.

## Ethical rules

1. Publish only evidence derived from the article or transformed into public summaries.
2. Do not publish confidential operational information.
3. Do not publish personal data or financial data.
4. Do not publish source code of institutional systems.
5. Do not publish security configurations that could help attack real systems.
6. Prefer synthetic examples over real extracts.
7. Keep academic reproducibility without exposing the entity.

## Review rule

Before committing new material, apply the publication checklist in `governance/publication-checklist.md`.
